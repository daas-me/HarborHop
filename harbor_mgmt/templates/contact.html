Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ferry ticket?

Simply visit our homepage, select your origin and destination, choose your travel dates, and follow the booking process.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your reservation through the "My Bookings" page. Cancellation policies vary by ferry operator.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, GCash, PayMaya, and over-the-counter payments at authorized centers.

How early should I arrive at the port?

We recommend arriving at least 1 hour before your scheduled departure time for check-in and boarding.
