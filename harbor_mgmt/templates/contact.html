Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a ferry ticket?

Simply visit our homepage, select your origin and destination, choose your travel dates, and follow the booking process. It's quick and easy!

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your reservation through the "Manage Booking" page. Cancellation policies vary by ferry operator.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit cards, debit cards, GCash, PayMaya, and over-the-counter payments at authorized centers.

How early should I arrive at the port?

We recommend arriving at least 2 hours before your scheduled departure time for check-in and boarding procedures.
